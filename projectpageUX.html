UX for Good

Helping New Orleans Musicians Thrive: Networking and Career Management Tools was a team-based practicum through UX for Good.

While there are plenty of existing small-business tools, after interviews with musicians and recording professionals, our team realized that a large obstacle to using these tools is many artists' ingrained believes that they are not good at dealing with money-- that their brains "don't work like that." We sought to incorporate Cognitive Behavioral Therapy into a prototype application to help the musicians network and organize their finances.
